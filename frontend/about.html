About The Shop

Samridhi Enterprises is a dedicated banking and financial service provider committed to making essential banking functions accessible to all, especially those facing challenges with traditional bank access. Founded and managed by Satyendra Kumar, Samridhi operates as a Customer Service Banking (CSB) center, meeting local banking needs with convenient, secure, and community-oriented services.

Our mission is to bring vital financial services directly to people, making everyday banking accessible without the need for a bank branch visit. This includes the Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS), which allows customers to make transactions securely using their Aadhaar-linked bank accounts and fingerprint verification.

We also offer credit card cash withdrawal and fingerprint-based systems, ensuring secure and convenient options for all customers. Satyendra Kumar and his team emphasize customer service, ensuring that each interaction is professional, friendly, and efficient.
